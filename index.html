About Ethel

Ethel Jena brings over two decades of service excellence in humanitarian and development work across South Africa. As the Safety and Security Manager for Peace Corps South Africa, she ensures the wellbeing of volunteers through proactive risk assessment, stakeholder coordination, and culturally grounded emergency preparedness.

Appointment Announcement

With over a decade of experience supporting Peace Corps Volunteers - including as one of the first Sexual Assault Response Liaisons - Ethel Jena was appointed Safety and Security Manager for Peace Corps South Africa.

Performance Evaluation

In her first months as Safety and Security Manager, Ethel was commended for her calm leadership style, timely responsiveness to Volunteer needs, and meaningful engagement across provinces. Her performance reflects a strong grasp of both the strategic and human dimensions of safety management.
