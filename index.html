About Ethel

With more than 15 years safeguarding people, programs, and partnerships, Ethel Jena is a seasoned Safety & Security professional with a reputation for balancing strong protocols with human care. She has built her career at the intersection of emergency preparedness, law-enforcement liaison, and volunteer support - ensuring that safety systems are not only compliant, but trusted and lived by those they protect.

Career Direction

Ethel is now pursuing senior roles such as Safety Officer, Security Manager, or Risk & Compliance Specialist, where her expertise in crisis response, incident reporting, and multi-stakeholder coordination can strengthen organizational resilience. Her professional focus is on creating environments where staff, partners, and communities feel both protected and empowered.
